# Launch Your Own Collected

## Zeit Now

1. Sign up to Now. You can start with the open source plan.
2. Sign up or into GitHub, and fork the [collected-systems](https://github.com/RoyalIcing/collected-systems) repo.
3. Sign up to Amazon Web Services. We recommend a new account **without** access to anything important already.
4. Deploy!
5. Alias to either a subdomain, or your own domain.
